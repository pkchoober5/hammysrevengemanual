📖 Hammy’s Revenge - Official Game Manual

🌟 Story

In a once-peaceful land, goblins have risen and ancient temples stir with dark energy. You are Hammy, a brave adventurer destined to restore balance and uncover the secrets of the Lost Temple.

🕹 Getting Started

  • Press New Game to begin your journey.
  • Follow Gramps' advice and explore Nibbler's Forest to find your first weapon.
  • Level up and prepare to challenge deeper dungeons and tougher foes!

🎮 Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Attack: Z or Left Click
  • Interact: E or Left Click
  • Inventory: I
  • Pause: ESC
  • Save: P

⚔️ Items & Equipment

Defeat goblins to earn loot and upgrade your gear. Equipment slots include:

  • Weapon
  • Armor
  • Pants
  • Hat
  • Ring

Tip: Loot drop chances vary. For example, Goblin Helmet has a higher drop rate than Goblin Mail.

👾 Enemies

Watch out for goblins in the wild. They patrol their zones and will chase you if you get too close.

  • Each goblin has a level, HP, and attack behavior.
  • Enemies respawn after time passes if the zone is revisited.

🗺 Zones & Exploration

Explore different zones by walking to the edge of the screen:

  • Nibbler's Forest – Where it all begins.
  • Lost Woods – Find the hidden temple entrance.
  • Muddy Meadow – A tougher area crawling with goblins.

Find doors, temples, and houses to unlock new areas and quests!

📜 Quests

Track your progress through quests like:

  • Wooden Arsenal: Find the Wooden Sword.
  • Become Stronger: Reach level 2.
  • Explore the Lost Temple: Enter the ancient ruins.

💡 Tips & Secrets

  • Try attacking with Z near goblins when you have a weapon equipped.
  • Press “E” near NPCs and doors to interact.
  • Save often! Press “P” to save your game.
  • Leveling up increases your HP, MP, and strength.
